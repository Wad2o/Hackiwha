Hook psychology, part 1: Curiosity Gap, Problem Amplification, Authority Challenge, Transformation

Curiosity Gap: the gap between what's known and what's wanted activates the
brain's dopamine reward-anticipation circuit. Weak if too vague ("I tried
something") — must be specific.

Problem Amplification: exploits loss aversion; avoided pain motivates more
than gained pleasure. Must name the precise symptom, not the general
category.

Authority Challenge: activates threat evaluation and belief updating;
conflict can't be ignored. Risky: without credible proof behind it, the
backlash is worse than not posting at all.

Transformation / Before-After: aspirational desire, same mechanism as
makeover shows. Works best with a concrete numeric contrast.
