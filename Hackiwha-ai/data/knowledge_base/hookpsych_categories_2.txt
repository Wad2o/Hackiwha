Hook psychology, part 2: Relatability, Urgency, Contradiction, Specificity Effect

Relatability / POV: social validation, confirms a shared experience.
Generates the most comments; validation test: "would someone tag a friend
under this?"

Urgency / FOMO: scarcity heuristic — what's limited or insider feels more
valuable. Manufactured urgency erodes trust over time.

Contradiction & Contrast: creates unresolved tension the brain can't let go
of without a response.

Specificity Effect: a bizarrely precise detail creates instant credibility
signal ("this person really lived this"). Must speak to one single person,
not an audience.
