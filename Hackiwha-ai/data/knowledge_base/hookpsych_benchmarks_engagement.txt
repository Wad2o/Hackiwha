Hook psychology: 12-month A/B benchmark, engagement rate by hook type

Data from a test where only the hook changes, identical content, posted on
Instagram/X/Reddit:

- "I spent $X and learned Y": 8.4% (best score)
- "Unpopular opinion:": 7.1%
- "Nobody talks about...": 4.2%
- "Here is what worked / what did not": 3.9%
- "X mistakes I made...": 3.1%
- "The truth about...": 2.4%
- "How to...": 1.8% (lowest score, seen as dated in 2026 and associated with
  paid-course imagery)

Additional insight: replying to all first-hour comments doubled reach on
identical content — the hook is not the only lever, first-hour activity
matters just as much.
