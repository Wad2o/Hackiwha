Hook psychology: 30-day tracking of 30 hook styles (~180 videos)

Data collected across combined TikTok/Reels/Shorts:

- Overall average across all categories: 4,700 views (median 3,100 views).
- Controversial Pattern Interrupt: 14,300 views average, 73% 3-second
  retention — best score, roughly 3x the average.
- Ultra-Specific Promise: 12,100 views average, 68% 3-second retention —
  roughly 2.5x the average.
- Generic question (e.g. "Are you struggling with X?"): only 2,400 views,
  18% 15-second retention — clearly below average.
