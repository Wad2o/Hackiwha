Hook psychology, community debate: do these patterns only work for the growth/marketing niche?

Unresolved in the corpus. One argument holds these hooks only work because
growth/marketing audiences already want more views, biasing the
measurement. A counter-argument cites examples outside that niche (physical
therapy, language learning) with unverified numbers (roughly 40% of
high-retention Reels analyzed reportedly came from non-marketing accounts,
and changing the hook alone reportedly added +20-30% watch time).
Calibration note: this counter-argument remains anecdotal, not a hard
dataset — treat with caution.

Related debate: niche specificity beats generic pattern. A hook that works
in finance doesn't necessarily work in postpartum fitness. Test patterns
within your own niche rather than copying one that worked elsewhere
verbatim, even if the underlying psychological mechanism seems transferable.
