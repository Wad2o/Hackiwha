Hook psychology: quick self-administered tests before publishing

- Hook test: would the first second alone make someone stop scrolling? If
  not, the hook isn't finished.
- Relatability test: would someone tag a friend under this post? A "hmm
  interesting" reaction isn't enough — there must be a concrete urge to
  share.
- Thumb-stop rule: interesting visual + engaging music + a fast, clear hook
  together is what physically stops the scrolling thumb.
