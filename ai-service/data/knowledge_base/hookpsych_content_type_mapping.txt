Hook psychology: content type -> recommended hook pattern mapping

- Tutorial / How-to -> Problem Amplification, Transformation. The viewer
  must feel the problem before wanting the solution.
- Product test -> Curiosity Gap, Urgency. Builds suspense around the
  verdict.
- Vlog / Day in the life -> Relatability, POV. Viewers stay for content
  reflecting their own experience.
- Hot take / Opinion -> Authority Challenge. Conflict draws attention.
- Storytime -> Curiosity Gap, Transformation. Sets up a narrative arc the
  viewer must finish.
- Limited offer / Launch -> Urgency, FOMO. Natural scarcity makes urgency
  authentic and credible.
