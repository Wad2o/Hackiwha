Hook psychology: source reliability notes

- Cited figures come from individual Reddit posts, unverified and not
  peer-reviewed: treat as practitioner heuristics, never as established
  statistical truths.
- A near-identical duplicate of the "1000+ viral hooks analyzed" post was
  identified (published twice, nearly identical text), as was a duplicate of
  the "47 hooks" guide reposted almost verbatim elsewhere — deduplicated in
  the dataset, mentioned here for transparency.
- Two source posts mentioned commercial tools (a "Free Viral Hook
  Generator", a dashboard named "Crescitaly") — excluded from the dataset as
  product placement in the source material, not genuine hook knowledge.
