Hook psychology: checklist of hook killers (eliminate systematically)

- Slow intro ("Hey guys, so today I wanted to talk about...").
- Payoff buried at the end of the video instead of teased upfront.
- First frame that looks like every other frame in the feed, nothing
  distinctive.
- No text overlay in the first second — many people watch muted.
- Manufactured urgency or fake scarcity.
- Authority challenge launched without credible proof behind it.
- Vulnerability that sounds "rehearsed" — audiences detect performed
  authenticity versus genuine spontaneous thought.
